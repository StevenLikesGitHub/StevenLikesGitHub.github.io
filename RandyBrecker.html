Randy Brecker

You can tell who has put in the time

Randy Brecker is an influential jazz and funk trumpeter responsible for being one of two leads of the fusion band The Brecker Bros. Starting in the mid-1970s, Brecker was partly responsible for pushing fusion and the popularity of jazz that made way for entertainers including Richard Elliot, Dave Koz, and David Sanborn's rise in the 1980s.
